About Me

I'm obsessed with being a user focused problem solver. Always being curious, digging through data, and talking with users guide my decision making. I have a do first ask forgiveness later attitude. I make mistakes quickly and clean them up even quicker. I hustle my way through problems until a solution is found. When I don’t know something, I figure it out. I’m never afraid to ask for help but make sure I do my homework first. I take the box, use the ideas inside of it and then tear it apart to find better ones on the outside. I make things happen.
